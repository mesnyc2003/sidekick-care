Product narrative

Built from real-world support experience

This concept is grounded in daily help desk work and direct experience helping older adults with technology. The product is not just “AI support.” It is a calmer, more accessible digital front door for people who often feel overwhelmed by basic tech tasks.

Why judges may care

The contest emphasizes usefulness, execution, clarity, creativity, and polish. This concept is easy to understand, solves a real need, is demo-friendly, and feels like a thoughtful product rather than a generic AI wrapper.
