Conversation-first support design

Technology help that feels more like a patient guide than a support ticket.

Willow Assist is built for older adults who feel overwhelmed by everyday tech problems. Instead of dashboards, clutter, and technical wording, it offers a calm conversation, clearer next steps, and a graceful handoff to family or staff when extra help is needed.

The problem

Older adults do not need “more tech.” They need calmer guidance.

Many support tools assume confidence, speed, and technical language. Willow is built for the opposite moment: when someone feels unsure, embarrassed, or afraid to press the wrong thing.

Why this wins

It feels like a real product with a market, not a school prototype.

Willow has a clear user, clear problem, differentiated interface, accessible design choices, and a demo that shows both emotional value and operational value for support teams.
