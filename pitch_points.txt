Pitch direction for Sidekick Care

1. Start with the problem
Older adults often struggle with everyday tech issues, but most support tools are too technical, too cluttered, or too stressful.

2. Explain why you built it
I built this from real-world experience helping older adults with common technology problems and seeing how often confusion, not complexity, is the real barrier.

3. Describe the product
Sidekick Care is an AI-powered accessibility-first support platform with guided troubleshooting, caregiver support, and structured handoff summaries for staff.

4. Explain why it stands out
It is not just another chatbot. It is designed around trust, accessibility, and real support workflows.

5. Demo path
- switch modes
- choose an issue
- show guided help
- show accessibility toggles
- generate the escalation summary

6. Close strong
This product makes support calmer for users and more efficient for the people helping them.
