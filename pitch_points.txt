Pitch structure

1. Start with the human problem
Older adults often struggle with everyday tech, and most support tools make that worse by feeling too technical or overwhelming.

2. Explain why you built it
I built Willow Assist from real experience helping seniors with technology and seeing how much calmer, clearer guidance could make a difference.

3. Describe the product
Willow Assist is a conversation-first support experience with real-life scenarios, simpler wording, accessibility controls, and a live helper handoff panel.

4. Show the wow factor
- real-life scenario cards
- empathetic chat design
- “I’m confused” button
- helper handoff note updating live

5. Close
It makes support feel more human for older adults and more efficient for the people helping them.
